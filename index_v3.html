Root cause
Why this keeps happening
You have injured yourself twice in the same position: lumbar flexion under ballistic load during fatigue. KB swings and devil presses require glutes to fire explosively. Under fatigue your glutes are too slow, erectors compensate, and your L4/L5 disc absorbs the shear force. That is the pop. This is gluteal amnesia under dynamic load — entirely fixable.

Avoid until Phase 4

  • Kettlebell swings — your exact injury mechanism, both times
  • Devil presses — overhead + hinge under fatigue, maximum risk
  • Loaded spinal flexion under fatigue: sit-ups, Russian twists, GHD
  • Sprint intervals while still symptomatic post-run
  • Barbell deadlifts from the floor until Phase 3 criteria are met
